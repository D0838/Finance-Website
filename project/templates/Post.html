Post Office Investment Schemes

Post Office Investment Schemes

Post Office Savings Account

The Post Office Savings Account is a basic savings account that offers a low-risk investment option. It provides easy liquidity and can be opened with a minimum balance.

  • Minimum balance: ₹500
  • Interest rate: 4% per annum
  • Tax benefits under Section 80TTA of the Income Tax Act

Recurring Deposit (RD)

The Post Office Recurring Deposit (RD) allows individuals to make regular deposits and earn a guaranteed return. It is suitable for those who want to build a corpus over time.

  • Minimum deposit: ₹100 per month
  • Interest rate: 5.8% per annum (compounded quarterly)
  • Tenure: 5 years

Monthly Income Scheme (MIS)

The Post Office Monthly Income Scheme (MIS) is ideal for individuals seeking a regular monthly income. It offers a fixed monthly payout and is a low-risk investment.

  • Minimum deposit: ₹1,000
  • Interest rate: 6.6% per annum
  • Tenure: 5 years

Public Provident Fund (PPF)

The Public Provident Fund (PPF) is a long-term investment option with attractive interest rates and tax benefits. It is a government-backed scheme aimed at small savings and income tax savings.

  • Minimum deposit: ₹500 per year
  • Interest rate: 7.1% per annum (compounded annually)
  • Tenure: 15 years
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act

National Savings Certificate (NSC)

The National Savings Certificate (NSC) is a fixed-income investment scheme that encourages savings among investors. It is suitable for risk-averse investors looking for a guaranteed return.

  • Minimum investment: ₹1,000
  • Interest rate: 6.8% per annum (compounded annually)
  • Tenure: 5 years
  • Tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act

Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP)

The Kisan Vikas Patra (KVP) is a savings certificate scheme that doubles the investment amount over a predetermined period. It is designed for individuals who want to invest in a safe and long-term instrument.

  • Minimum investment: ₹1,000
  • Interest rate: 6.9% per annum (compounded annually)
  • Maturity period: 124 months
